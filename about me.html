marie van der linden



I am an artist (b. 2004, based in Rotterdam, The Netherlands) focused on photography, text, illustration and metalwork. The themes I use for my work are most often about sex and sexuality, identity and queerness, social structures as well as death/life cycles/consciousness. I try to find a certain point of truth in using and borrowing from my own dream images, studies on metaphysics and the intelligence of the body or nature. Through these ways of research, I question the rules and systems we live under.



I often use myself as a testing ground. By meditating/masturbating/sleeping I reflect on the effect the outside world full of hate and injustice and chaos and restraint has on me, and document this.



It is important to me to work from a DIY mentality finding, borrowing, working together, basically cutting and taping things together. Being in person and exchanging resources is an important part of my practice. I work in social contexts, with squats, on the streets, queercafe’s. Through showing my work, I want to go into discussion, break taboos around sex, queerness and grief -> to create cohesion, understanding and education, while centering accessibility and care.



Contact me if u want to work together on an exhibition, workshop, reading group, publication or something else !



( education: Photography at Willem de Kooning Academie, Rotterdam, The Netherlands, 2024-2028 )
